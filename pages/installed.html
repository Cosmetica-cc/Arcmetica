You've found Arcmetica!

Looks like it's installed and everything's good to go.

Arcmetica works by redirecting requests from OptiFine's cosmetics server to our own. This method lets us show drippy cosmetics in almost any Minecraft version that has OptiFine installed!

Boot up Minecraft using OptiFine and check out your drippy new cosmetics! If you want to uninstall Arcmetica, you can find instructions to do so here or you can join our Discord server.
